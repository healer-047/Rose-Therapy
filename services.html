Who it’s for

Therapy can be helpful if you’re feeling overwhelmed, stuck in patterns you want to change, navigating a relationship challenge, or seeking more clarity and calm.

  • You want tools to manage stress, worry, or low mood.
  • You want to improve communication, boundaries, or self‑esteem.
  • You want a supportive space to process difficult experiences at a safe pace.

What to expect

  • A warm, professional conversation—with clear structure.
  • Collaborative goals (you can change them anytime).
  • Practical strategies and reflection between sessions (optional).

Therapy is not a crisis service. If you’re in immediate danger, contact local emergency services.

Anxiety

Anxiety can show up as constant worry, physical tension, racing thoughts, or avoidance. Together we’ll explore what fuels the cycle and build calmer, more flexible responses.

  • Understanding triggers and thought patterns
  • Grounding tools and nervous system regulation
  • Gentle exposure and confidence‑building (where appropriate)

Depression / low mood

When motivation and hope feel low, small steps matter. We’ll work with compassion and structure, focusing on routines, meaning, and supportive coping skills.

  • Rebuilding supportive habits and daily rhythm
  • Working with self‑critical thinking and shame
  • Strengthening connection and activity (at your pace)

Stress / burnout

Burnout is often a sign that your needs and limits have been stretched too far for too long. We focus on boundaries, recovery, and sustainable change.

  • Reducing overload and identifying what drains you
  • Boundary setting and values‑based choices
  • Recovery planning you can maintain

Relationship issues

Relationships can bring comfort and strain. Therapy can help you communicate more clearly, understand attachment patterns, and strengthen boundaries.

  • Communication and conflict repair
  • Boundaries and self‑respect
  • Understanding repeating dynamics

Trauma‑informed support

Trauma‑informed work prioritises safety, consent, pacing, and stabilisation. We focus on helping you feel more grounded before exploring difficult material.

  • Stabilising tools (sleep, grounding, regulation)
  • Understanding trauma responses without judgement
  • Paced processing only when you feel ready

Life transitions

Change can bring grief, uncertainty, or identity shifts. We support you in finding footing, making sense of what’s happening, and moving forward with intention.

  • Decision‑making and clarity
  • Managing uncertainty and fear of change
  • Building self‑trust and resilience
