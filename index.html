How it works

A simple, respectful process designed to reduce friction.

  1. 1

    Choose a preferred time

    Select an available slot in UK time (we’ll show your local time too).

  2. 2

    Send your request via WhatsApp

    Your details are pre‑filled. Review, edit if needed, and press send.

  3. 3

    We confirm and share next steps

    We’ll confirm a time, share payment details, and answer any questions—no pressure.

What happens after you message us?

  • We’ll reply with availability confirmation (or alternatives if needed).

  • You’ll receive a video link and a short welcome note before your session.
